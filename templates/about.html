Description:

Basic Artificial Garbage Sorting, also known as B.A.G.S, is a OpenCV and Spaghetti powered website designed to detect plastic bags amongst recyclable materials. Our use case is for recycling plants that need to throw away large amounts of recyclable material because of trash pollution. Our website is meant to help recycling plants pick common articles of trash, like plastic bags, out of recyclable materials that are still viable for reuse. Our software is expandable, and we plan on later adding detection for other common articles of trash like plastic straws and bubble wrap.
